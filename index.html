Mission Statement

Our mission is to be the best New York Bird Sanctuary and life-care facility for captive exotic birds. In particular, we are a refuge for Hyacinth Macaws, Cockatoos, Green Wings, African Greys, Eclectus, Sun Conures and other exotic birds. Zaksee Parrot Sanctuary is an important step in helping unwanted, unloved and abused parrots.
